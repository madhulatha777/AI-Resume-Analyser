Senior Backend Engineer (Python)

About the Role
We are looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to design and scale our cloud
platform. You will own services end to end, from API design to deployment on
AWS, and mentor junior engineers.

Responsibilities
- Design, build, and maintain scalable REST and gRPC APIs in Python.
- Deploy and operate services on AWS using Docker and Kubernetes.
- Build CI/CD pipelines and improve observability.
- Optimize PostgreSQL queries and data models for performance.
- Mentor junior engineers and lead code reviews.

Required Qualifications
- 5+ years of professional Python experience.
- Strong experience with FastAPI or Django REST Framework.
- Hands-on experience with AWS (ECS/EKS, S3, RDS, Lambda).
- Proficiency with Docker and Kubernetes.
- Solid understanding of PostgreSQL and database design.
- Experience with CI/CD (GitHub Actions, Jenkins) and infrastructure as code.

Nice to Have
- Experience with Kafka or other message queues.
- Familiarity with Terraform.
- Knowledge of monitoring tools (Prometheus, Grafana, Datadog).

Education
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent experience.
